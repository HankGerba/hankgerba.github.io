Hank Gerba

Updated August 2024

Profile

Hank is a media theorist specializing computation, AI, and aesthetics. They recently received their PhD from Stanford University's Film & Media Program in the Art & Art History Department. Their dissertation, "Digital Disruptions: Moiré, Aliasing, and the Stroboscopic Effect"https://searchworks.stanford.edu/view/in00000106660 recasts digital artifacts as images, and in so doing constructs a digital aesthetics founded on oscillations between analog and digital processes. Their work has been published in Real Life and Media-N, and is forthcoming elsewhere.

Outside of academia, Hank co-produced the Emmy-nominated VR Short Wonder Buffalo: The VR Experience,https://vimeo.com/224432295 wrote for and operated Sophia the Robot,https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sophia and has expertise in the functional relationship between AI and narrative based on many collaborations at the University of Southern California's Entertainment Technology Center.https://cinema.usc.edu/etc/
