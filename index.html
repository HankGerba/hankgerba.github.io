Hank is a media theorist specializing computation,tbd AI, and aesthetics.tbd They are currently finishing their PhD at Stanford University in the Art & Art History Department's Film & Media Program. Their dissertation uses the phenomenon of aliasinghttps://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aliasing to reconceptualize what it means to be embodied in light of computational systems, images, and intelligences.
